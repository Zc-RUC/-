# Daily Paper Briefing Skill

## Purpose

Daily Paper Briefing Skill is designed to monitor newly released papers in target research areas and convert them into concise daily updates.

It helps recruiting, research, and investment teams track frontier topics and discover relevant authors faster.

## Typical Inputs

- Target venues
- Target keywords
- arXiv or conference feed
- Research area list
- Candidate sourcing criteria

## Typical Outputs

- Daily paper list
- One-line summary for each paper
- Research area tag
- Author information
- Potential candidate notes
- Follow-up priority

## Workflow

1. Monitor new papers from selected venues or feeds.
2. Filter papers by target research areas.
3. Summarize the topic, method, and potential relevance.
4. Extract author information and connect it to sourcing workflows.
5. Deliver a daily briefing for review.

## Use Case

Used with Feishu Aime and Mira to automate daily paper updates and author information delivery for AI Infra recruiting.
