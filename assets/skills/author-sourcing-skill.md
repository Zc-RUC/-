# Author Sourcing Skill

## Purpose

Author Sourcing Skill is designed to identify, verify, and enrich paper authors from top systems and AI conferences for recruiting and talent research.

It starts from papers, resolves authors to real profiles, and produces recruiter-ready candidate information.

## Typical Inputs

- Paper title
- Conference or venue
- Author list
- Paper URL or PDF
- Target research fields

## Typical Outputs

- Author identity
- Current affiliation
- Personal homepage
- GitHub profile
- Google Scholar profile
- Advisor or lab
- Research direction
- Candidate relevance notes
- YES / NO / MAYBE sourcing judgment

## Workflow

1. Parse paper metadata and author list.
2. Resolve same-name ambiguity through affiliation, homepage, papers, and advisor information.
3. Prefer official personal pages, university pages, lab pages, and publication databases.
4. Enrich each author with homepage, GitHub, Google Scholar, advisor, and current affiliation.
5. Summarize fit for recruiting or research follow-up.

## Use Case

Used to review authors from venues such as EuroSys, ASPLOS, and FAST, and convert paper author lists into structured sourcing leads.
